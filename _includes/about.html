Small beginnings...

Bass Raft comes to you via an upstart father and son team of Wisconsin fisherman with a different vision for kayak fishing. We've built a mashup of bass boat deck and kayak, a super lightweight and stable platform designed so you can fish all day standing up.

A Sunday afternoon conversation about composite construction turned into an obsession to build the best fishing platform the world has ever seen. If you forget that you are NOT on the deck of a boat, lightly cruising around a lake, through shallows, quietly creeping up on a dock, you know we've succeeded in our goal.
