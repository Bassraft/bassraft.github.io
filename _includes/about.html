The Bass Raft Story

Bass Raft began as a casual Sunday conversation between my father and uncle, exploring the ideas of crafting a composite fishing platform. This led to the development of the first Bass Raft prototype in July of 2021. We loved it!

I was inspired to design and build a new Bass Raft for my high school senior project in May of 2022, culminating in the launch of my own business... Bass Raft in March of 2024.

Stand up and fish,

Joey


Bass Raft is proudly manufactured in the USA, in west central Wicsonsin.
Each Bass Raft is hand crafted and precision tuned to be light and strong, ready for as many days on the water as you are.
